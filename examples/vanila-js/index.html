JanusJs Vanilla Demos

Feature-complete walk-throughs for the Janus plugins bundled with typed_janus_js. Use these pages to verify WebRTC flows without relying on the legacy Janus demo UI.

Available demos

VideoRoom Publish, subscribe and manage feeds. AudioBridge Spatial audio, chat and participant controls. Streaming Watch and control live relays. SIP Softphone with call management. Video Call Direct call, screen-share and call controls.
